The Diet of a Stoic

Yo, Stoics kept it real with food—simple, natural, and no drama. They ate to live, not the other way around.

What They Ate

Think fruits, veggies, grains, maybe some cheese or milk. Nothing fancy—just what’s around and keeps you solid.

Why It Matters

It’s all about self-control, bro. Eat what fuels you, not what owns you. Stay chill and focused.

Want more? Check old-school Stoics like Marcus Aurelius or Musonius Rufus—they’d vibe with this.
